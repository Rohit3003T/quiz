Question 1: Number Series (Growing Differences)

4, 11, 25, 53, 109, ?
A) 163
B) 215
C) 221
D) 225
💡 Step-by-Step Solution:
Step 1: Find differences between consecutive terms:
11-4=7, 25-11=14, 53-25=28, 109-53=56
Step 2: Analyze the differences: 7, 14, 28, 56
Notice: 14=7×2, 28=14×2, 56=28×2 (Each doubles!)
Step 3: Next difference = 56×2 = 112
Final answer: 109 + 112 = 221
🎯 Pro Trick:
Always check if differences form a pattern (doubling, tripling, arithmetic progression)!
✅ Answer: C (221)

Question 2: Letter–Number Pattern

A1, C4, F9, J16, O25, ?
A) U36
B) V36
C) W36
D) X49
💡 Dual Pattern Analysis:
Letters: A(1) → C(3) → F(6) → J(10) → O(15) → U(21)
Position jumps: +2, +3, +4, +5, +6 (incremental pattern!)
Numbers: 1, 4, 9, 16, 25, 36
Pattern: 1²=1, 2²=4, 3²=9, 4²=16, 5²=25, 6²=36 (Perfect squares!)
Combination: U (21st letter) + 36 (6²) = U36
🎯 Pro Trick:
For mixed patterns, solve letters and numbers separately, then combine!
✅ Answer: A (U36)

Question 3: Number Series (Odd Increments)

2, 5, 10, 17, 26, 37, ?
A) 46
B) 48
C) 49
D) 50
💡 Odd Number Pattern:
Step 1: Calculate differences:
5-2=3, 10-5=5, 17-10=7, 26-17=9, 37-26=11
Step 2: Differences are: 3, 5, 7, 9, 11
Pattern: Consecutive odd numbers! (+2 each time)
Step 3: Next odd number = 11 + 2 = 13
Final answer: 37 + 13 = 50
🎯 Pro Trick:
Odd/even number sequences are super common - memorize them: 1,3,5,7,9,11,13...
✅ Answer: D (50)
